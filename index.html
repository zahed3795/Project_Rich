About The Founder

Precision, Trust, and Legacy Building

Isbah Wealth & Immigration was founded on the core belief that securing your family's future requires strategic planning and an intimate understanding of our community's unique needs.

As the Founder and Lead Consultant, Zahed Khan brings a unique skill set tailored to Houston's diverse landscape. Understanding the nuances of cross-border families, first-generation home buying, and culturally conscious investing, Zahed applies a meticulous approach to every financial portfolio, tax document, and immigration form.
